Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

GoSpend ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple user identifier and, if you choose to share them, your name and email address.

Budgeting Data: Information you enter into the app, including:

  • Income and payday settings
  • Spending amounts and categories
  • Budget goals and preferences
  • Recurring transaction details

Subscription Information: Your subscription status (free or premium) is managed through RevenueCat. We do not see or store your payment details.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Bank account or credit card numbers
  • Financial account login credentials
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise your budgeting experience
  • To sync your data across your devices
  • To generate spending insights and tips
  • To manage your premium subscription

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with encryption at rest and in transit. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign In — authentication
  • Supabase — secure cloud database
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (they do not receive your budgeting data)
  • Apple App Store — payment processing

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you have an active account. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app's Settings page. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

GoSpend is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data from the app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@gospend.app
